Karonmajigi Community Outreach

Saturday June 8, 2024 | Karonmajigi Primary Health Centre, FCT Nigeria

The Faith Sisters Global Mission Healthcare Outreach took place at the Karonmajigi Primary Health Centre on Saturday June 8, 2024, for the Karonmajigi community.

The Faith Sisters Global Mission addressed critical needs within the Karonmajigi community healthcare center, such as solving the drinking water challenge and mosquito infestation. Thanks to the donations from the members of Faith Sisters Global Mission and our partners, we provided essential items to the healthcare center, including a water dispenser, a first aid box, free medication, a blood pressure monitor, and a body scale.

We also distributed mosquito nets, wipes, and diapers to over 250 community members. Additionally, we educated the community on safety measures, promoted a healthy lifestyle, and taught them about wholeness in Christ Jesus. The feedback we received from the community members was truly heartwarming, and they were thankful for the significant impact on their lives in Karonmajigi, FCT Nigeria.

Story Highlights

  • The outreach addressed key health centre needs, including drinking water challenges and mosquito infestation.
  • Essential supplies included a water dispenser, first aid box, free medication, blood pressure monitor, and body scale.
  • Over 250 community members received mosquito nets, wipes, and diapers, alongside education on safety, healthy living, and wholeness in Christ Jesus.
