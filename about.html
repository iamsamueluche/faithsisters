About Us

Discover our story, mission, and the values that drive us to make a difference.

Who We Are

A Sisterhood United by Faith

Faith Sisters Global Mission is a faith-based nonprofit organization founded on January 1, 2020. We are a diverse group of women united by our shared commitment to uplifting one another and fostering spiritual growth in Christ Jesus.

We gather together every first Saturday of the month for prayer, Bible study, and mutual edification. Our mission is to provide a safe and welcoming space where women from all walks of life can come together to explore and deepen their spiritual connections, share their stories, and find strength and inspiration in the collective wisdom of our diverse sisterhood.

Through our prayer meetings, outreaches, online platforms, and resources, we strive to empower women to embrace their spirituality, cultivate mindfulness, and live with purpose and authenticity.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To nurture spiritual growth, foster sisterhood, and spread love and compassion to all. We are dedicated to providing a nurturing environment where individuals from all walks of life can come together to explore and strengthen their faith, find support during challenging times, and celebrate the joys of life's journey.

Through our various outreach programs, we aim to touch lives and transform communities with the love of Christ, making a tangible difference in the lives of those we serve.

Our Vision

To be a global community of women living for Christ and taking the gospel to the nations. We envision a world where every woman has the opportunity to grow spiritually, find sisterhood, and live a life of purpose and authenticity.

We aspire to create a ripple effect of positive change, where empowered women empower others, creating a global movement of faith, love, and service that transforms communities and impacts generations.

Our Story

Our Journey

From a small group of women with a shared vision to a growing community making impact across Africa, our journey has been one of faith, growth, and transformation.

January 2020

Faith Sisters Global Mission was founded with a vision to create a community of women united by faith.

June 2020

Launched our first community outreach program, providing food and supplies to families in need.

December 2021

Expanded our reach to multiple communities across Nigeria, impacting over 500 lives.

March 2023

Launched our education support program, providing scholarships and school supplies to children.

Today

Continuing to grow and expand our impact, touching thousands of lives across Africa.
