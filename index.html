About Us

A Sisterhood Built on Faith, Love & Service

Faith Sisters Global Mission is a faith-based nonprofit organization founded on January 1, 2020. We are a diverse group of women united by our shared commitment to uplifting one another and fostering spiritual growth in Christ Jesus.

We gather together every first Saturday of the month for prayer, Bible study, and mutual edification. Through our prayer meetings, outreaches, online platforms, and resources, we strive to empower women to embrace their spirituality and live with purpose.

  • Monthly prayer meetings and Bible study
  • Community outreach programs across Africa
  • Education and healthcare support initiatives
  • Youth empowerment and leadership training
