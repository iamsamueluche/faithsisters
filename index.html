About Us
A Sisterhood Built on Faith & Love

Faith sisters was founded on January 1, 2020. We are a group of sisters who gather together every first Saturday of the month for prayer, bible study and mutual edification. Our mission is to provide a safe and welcoming space where women from all walks of life can come together to explore and deepen their spiritual connections, share their stories, and find strength and inspiration in the collective wisdom of our diverse sisterhood, through our prayer meetings, outreaches, online platforms and resources we strive to empower women to embrace their spirituality, cultivate mindfulness, and live with purpose and authenticity. Whether you’re seeking fellowship, guidance or solace, Faith Sisters is here to accompany you on your journey. Join us as we embark on a transformative voyage of faith, love, and sisterhood. Together, let's illuminate the world with our shared light and make a difference in the lives of others.
