JKS Orphanage Home Outreach

22nd November 2025 | JKS Special Needs Orphanage, Kubwa, Abuja

The Faith Sisters Global Mission visited the JKS Special Needs Orphanage in Kubwa, Abuja, for the Love in Action Outreach on 22nd November 2025.

It was a heartfelt time of sharing God's love with children with special needs through prayers, songs, warm interactions, and the donation of essential supplies.

The outreach strengthened our commitment to caring for special needs children and supporting the caregivers who serve them daily. We appreciate all Faith Sisters members, sponsors and partners whose contributions made the outreach impactful. Through Love in Action, we continue to demonstrate that true faith is expressed through compassion, service, and genuine community support.

Story Highlights

  • The outreach shared God's love with children with special needs through prayers, songs, and warm interactions.
  • Essential supplies were donated to support the children and the caregivers who serve them daily.
  • Love in Action reaffirmed the mission's commitment to compassion, service, and genuine community support.
