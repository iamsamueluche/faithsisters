JKS Orphanage Home Outreach

22nd November 2025 | JKS Special Needs Orphanage, Kubwa – Abuja

A compassion-led visit where service, presence, and encouragement were shared with children living in a special needs home.

This outreach reflected the heart of Love in Action: showing up in ways that make care visible. Through warm interaction, practical support, and intentional time spent with the children, the event created a day marked by dignity and belonging.

More than a visit, it became a reminder that meaningful outreach often begins with simple acts of presence. The atmosphere carried kindness, faith, and the reassurance that every child deserves to feel seen and valued.

Story Highlights

  • Compassion-centered orphanage outreach with direct community care.
  • Warm engagement, encouragement, and support shared with the children.
  • A day that expressed dignity, joy, and visible faith in action.
