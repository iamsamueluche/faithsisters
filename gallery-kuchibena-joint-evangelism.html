Kuchibena Joint Evangelism Outreach

Faith Sisters 2023 Outreach | Kuchibena with Coza CPC Gwarimpa

The Second Edition of Faith Sisters 2023 Outreach, was a joint evangelism with Coza CPC Gwarimpa at Kuchibena, This collaborative effort was a harmonious convergence of hearts within the body of Christ.

The warmth from over 260 people upon our arrival in Kuchibena Community was so beautiful to see 🌟❤ We had an awesome time of vibrant praise and intense worship, we passionately shared the life-transforming gospel of Jesus, we earnestly decreed healing, laying hands on the sick and extending comforting hands as we ministered materially to the people of Kuchibena.

we were deeply moved and humbled, recognizing the magnitude of the work that lies ahead. This experience reiterated the profound importance of each day spent in service to God and humanity. Our profound gratitude extends to Coza CPC Gwarimpa for their collaborative spirit. This outreach witnessed the immense potential when hearts unite for our Saviour Jesus Christ and testament of the beauty and Power found within it. we look ahead to continuing our journey, fervently believing in God's boundless grace to do more in spreading the love and message of Christ.

Story Highlights

  • A joint evangelism with Coza CPC Gwarimpa brought a powerful convergence of hearts within the body of Christ.
  • Over 260 people welcomed the team into Kuchibena Community with warmth, worship, praise, healing, and gospel ministry.
  • The outreach showed the beauty and power of unity in Christ and strengthened the mission to keep spreading His love.
