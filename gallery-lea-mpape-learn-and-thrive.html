Learn & Thrive Empowerment Outreach

September 2025 | LEA Primary School, Mpape Community, Abuja

A practical empowerment outreach designed to connect learning with real-life confidence and growth.

This event centered on skills acquisition as a tool for transformation. By creating a space where participants could engage, learn, and imagine new possibilities, the outreach moved beyond motivation into practical empowerment.

The day reflected Faith Sisters Global Mission’s commitment to helping communities thrive through support that is thoughtful, relevant, and future-facing. It was an outreach that joined encouragement with action.

Story Highlights

  • Skills-focused outreach with clear empowerment goals.
  • Strong engagement at LEA Primary School in Mpape Community.
  • An atmosphere that encouraged growth, confidence, and possibility.
