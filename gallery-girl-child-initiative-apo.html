Girl Child Initiative

March 9, 2024 | St. Anglican Girls School, Apo, Abuja

Our first inaugural outreach for 2024 occurred on March 9, 2024. The Education Program Outreach at St. Anglican Girls School in Apo, Abuja, themed "The Girl Child Initiative," educated 500 girls on the importance of cleanliness and godliness.

Our program featured interactive sessions focusing on menstrual health education, personal hygiene, and identity in Christ Jesus.In addition to our educational sessions, Faith Sisters Global provided the students with 360 sanitary pads, 50 pens, 50 jotters, and 350 educational materials to equip the girls with tools to track their menstrual flow and information on menstrual hygiene, along with light refreshments.

The feedback we received from the girls and school administrators was truly heartwarming. They expressed confidence in living healthier and more confident lives and were immensely grateful for the support they received through the generosity of Faith Sisters Global and its partner network.Faith Sisters Global is committed to empowering young girls and women through education and support, making a significant impact on their lives and contributing to the betterment of Africa. We're grateful to our sponsors and partners for their unwavering commitment, time, and resources.

Story Highlights

  • 500 girls were educated on cleanliness, godliness, menstrual health, personal hygiene, and identity in Christ Jesus.
  • Faith Sisters Global provided 360 sanitary pads, 50 pens, 50 jotters, 350 educational materials, and light refreshments.
  • The outreach strengthened confidence in the girls and reflected the mission's commitment to empowering young girls and women through education and support.
