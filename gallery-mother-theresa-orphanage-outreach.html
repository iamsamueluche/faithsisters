Mother Theresa Children's Home Visit

Saturday October 7, 2024 | St. Mother Theresa Children's Orphanage, Gwarimpa, Abuja

The inaugural outreach of 2024 by Faith Sisters Global took place at St. Mother Theresa Children's Orphanage in Gwarimpa, Abuja on Saturday October 7, 2024.

It was an incredibly impactful time of ministering to these children with gifts ranging from provisions, medicines, foodstuffs, words, and prayers.

Faith Sisters Global is dedicated to ensuring that orphans experience the love of Christ, and fulfill his commandment to welcome the little ones This moment serves as a reminder that we are created for a purpose greater than ourselves, to embody the love of Christ and serve humanity through the gifts He has bestowed upon us.

Story Highlights

  • The inaugural outreach of 2024 centered on children at St. Mother Theresa Children's Orphanage.
  • Provisions, medicines, foodstuffs, words, and prayers were shared in a deeply impactful time of ministry.
  • The outreach reflected the mission to embody Christ's love and welcome the little ones with compassion.
