Education Outreach – Reframing: Social-Emotional Learning | April 2026 | Government Girls Secondary School Dutse, Abuja, FCT, Nigeria

Saturday, 25th April 2026 | Government Girls Secondary School Dutse, Abuja, FCT, Nigeria

On Saturday, 25th April 2026, Faith Sisters Global Mission held an impactful Education Outreach at Government Girls Secondary School Dutse, bringing together young female students for a day of learning, inspiration, and transformation.

Centered on the theme “Reframing,” the outreach encouraged the girls to shift their perspectives about life, challenges, and their personal journeys. Through engaging sessions, they were taught how to renew their mindset, overcome limiting beliefs, and see themselves through the lens of purpose, faith, and possibility. The message emphasized that with the right mindset, every obstacle can become an opportunity for growth and advancement.

A major highlight of the outreach was the award of scholarships to twenty-five (25) deserving students. This initiative provided school fee support of ₦20,000 per student, amounting to a total of ₦500,000. The scholarship recognized not only academic effort but also resilience, determination, and the potential to succeed despite financial constraints. It served as a powerful reminder that their dreams are valid and achievable.

The outreach left a lasting impact on both the students and the school community, sparking renewed hope, confidence, and motivation among the girls.

Faith Sisters Global Mission remains deeply grateful to its dedicated members, partners, and sponsors whose unwavering support continues to drive meaningful change.

Event Highlights

  • The outreach centered on “Reframing,” helping the girls renew their mindset, overcome limiting beliefs, and see themselves through purpose, faith, and possibility.
  • Twenty-five (25) deserving students received scholarship support of ₦20,000 each, totaling ₦500,000 in school fee assistance.
  • The school community experienced renewed hope, confidence, and motivation through the support of dedicated members, partners, and sponsors.
