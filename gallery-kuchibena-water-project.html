Kuchibena Project

November 30, 2024 | Kuchibena Community Water Project

On November 30th 2024, the Faith Sisters Global Mission brought hope to over 1,000 members of the Kuchibena community by addressing the challenge of water scarcity.

The initiative involved drilling a borehole to provide free, clean, and sustainable water. Engaging the community head and members ensured the program's success and long-term sustainability.

The project empowered the Kuchibena community, reaffirming Faith Sisters Global's commitment to improving health and wellbeing in underserved areas across Africa.

Story Highlights

  • Over 1,000 members of the Kuchibena community benefited from the water project.
  • A borehole was drilled to provide free, clean, and sustainable water.
  • Community engagement supported the project's success, sustainability, and long-term health impact.
